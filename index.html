We are a University of North Texas (UNT) and Texas Academy of Math and Science (TAMS) official club dedicated to preparing members for a life in medicine and bring awareness of cutting-edge breakthroughs to members through collaborative activities and leadership opportunities.

Our primary goals for the 2018-2019 year include: 

  1. Better Prepare Members to Enter Medical Field

  2. Increase Consistent & Impactful Volunteering + Shadowing

  3. Establish three new committees and improve eight existing ones

  4. Increase attendance and donations at Aid The Cause + execute more guest speaker events

  5. Encourage increased appreciation of the medical humanities through a student published journal
